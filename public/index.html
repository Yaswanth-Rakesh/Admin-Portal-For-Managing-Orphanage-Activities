Providing Love and Care to Children in Need

At Sri Guru Raghavendra Orphanage, we are committed to providing shelter, education, and a loving family environment for orphaned and underprivileged children in Ananthapur.

About Our Orphanage

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing environment where orphaned and vulnerable children can receive love, care, education, and opportunities to develop into responsible citizens guided by the principles of Sri Guru Raghavendra Swamy.

Our Values

  • Compassion and empathy for every child
  • Commitment to quality education
  • Respect for individual differences
  • Community involvement and support
  • Transparency in all operations

Love & Care

We provide a family-like environment where every child feels loved, valued, and secure.

Education

We ensure all children receive quality education and learning opportunities.

Support

We offer continuous support even after children leave our care until they're fully independent.
